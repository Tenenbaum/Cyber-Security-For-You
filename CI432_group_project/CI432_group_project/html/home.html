Intro to Cyber Security

This site is designed to educate and inform you about cyber security. Cyber security is the techniques of protecting networks and computers. When going online we face many threats such as hacking, identity theft and viruses; it is essential that we stay protected from these dangers.

Our mission is to make your experience while browsing the internet much safer, as well as providing you with the relevant information to make you aware on how to stay safe while exploring the World Wide Web and staying protected from these potential threats.

Cyber security consists of technologies, processes and people. Technology is whereby you have the hardware and software to protect yourself from cybercrimes. Process is the management systems, how people and organizations can manage their technology and protect it. Finally, people; training people and kids about cyber security. Informing people and making them aware of the measures and precautions they can take.

“In 2018 alone, within 6 months there were 945 data breaches and 4.5 million records at risk”.

Kids in particular should really be educated about cyber security, especially in a day and age where kids are hooked on to technology. Whether you are on social media, watching videos or playing games. It is essential that you are aware of the hidden dangers behind technology such as hackers, cookies etc
